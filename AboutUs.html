ABOUT US

Hi, We're VANISHING TREASURES

We made this website to give attention to our animals who are endangered from all the human actions and natural occurrences. As a small project, we focused on Tarlac Province to show our visitors the current situation of our animal kingdom. We hope to give awareness for our visitors to show what we are missing or going to lose.
